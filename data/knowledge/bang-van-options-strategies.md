## Two Key Questions for Strategy Selection

1. Are you BULLISH, BEARISH or NEUTRAL?
2. Is Implied Volatility HIGH or LOW?

## Strategy Selection Grid

### HIGH IV + BULLISH (Sell Premium)
- Sell Cash Secured Put (CSP)
- Sell Covered Call (CC)
- Sell Bull Put Spread (BPS) / Bull Put Spread Xtreme (BPSX)
- Sell Jade Lizard (JL)

### HIGH IV + BEARISH (Sell Premium)
- Sell Bear Call Spread (BCS)

### HIGH IV + NEUTRAL (Sell Premium)
- Sell Iron Condor (IC)

### LOW IV + BULLISH (Buy Strategies)
- Buy Long Call
- Buy Bull Bang / Bull Bang Collar (BBC)
- Buy Bull Call Spread (BuCS)
- Buy Diagonal Spread (DS) / Diagonal Spread with Collar (BBC)
- Buy Crouching Bull Spread (CBS)
- Bullish Synthetic Spread (SS)

### LOW IV + BEARISH (Buy Strategies)
- Buy Long Put
- Buy Protective Put
- Buy Bear Put Spread (BePS)
- Bearish Synthetic Spread

### LOW IV + NEUTRAL
- Calendar Spread (CS) / Double Calendar Spread
- Earning Probability Spread (EPS)
- Earnings Sniper Spread (ESS)
- SNIPEX / SNIPEX 4B

### EARNINGS PLAYS (any IV, dual-directional)
- Earning Probability Spread (EPS)
- Earnings Sniper Spread (ESS)
- SNIPEX / SNIPEX 4B
- Calendar Spread

## Preferred Strategies & Securities (Bang Van's Picks)

### Bull Bang / Bull Bang Collar
- Security: Stocks with good fundamentals
- Rationale: Construct bullish trade with protection
  - Fundamentally good stocks on an uptrend
  - Bottom fishing: good stocks beaten down with imminent bounce/recovery

### Calendar Spread
- Security: Stocks with good premiums on weekly options
- Rationale: Various ways to trade (directional, non-directional, neutral, dual-directional, earning plays)
- Low cost, low risk

### Diagonal Spread / CSP / BPS / Covered Call / Crouching Bull / Jade Lizard
- Security: Fundamentally good stocks, good technical setup (neutral to bullish)
- Rationale:
  - Generate extra income (CC), lease to rent (DS)
  - Collect premiums free or own at discount (CSP, BPS)
  - Watch-out for BPS: avoid earnings/key events when price could gap significantly

### Earnings Sniper Spread (ESS)
- Security: High-liquid stocks with price > $80
- Rationale: Wide profit zone, no need to pick direction. IV likely increases before earnings = high prob trade

### SNIPEX
- Security: SPX only
- Rationale: High ROI potential, high probability

### Iron Condor
- Security: Futures (/CL, /6E, /GC, /NG, /ES) or Indices (SPX, NDX, RUT)
- Rationale: Pre-defined risk, low capital, good premiums. No individual stock risks.
- Watch-out: Commission intensive (4 legs)
